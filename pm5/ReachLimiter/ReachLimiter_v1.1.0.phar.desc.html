🛡️ ReachLimiter

Stop reach-hackers in their tracks with precision and performance.

ReachLimiter is a high-performance, lightweight anti-cheat solution for PocketMine-MP designed specifically to combat "Reach" hacks. It ensures that PvP remains fair by validating the distance of every hit using precise coordinate calculations.

🚀 Why ReachLimiter?

Many anti-cheats are bulky and cause lag. ReachLimiter focuses on one thing and does it perfectly: preventing unfair attack distances without sacrificing server resources.

✨ Key Features

  • 🎯 High Precision: Calculates distances based on eye-to-eye and body positions to ensure accuracy.
  • ⚡ Ultra Lightweight: Minimalist code logic that won't impact your TPS, even with 100+ players.
  • 🛠️ Fully Configurable: Set your own reach limits to match your server's gameplay style (Combo, Survival, etc.).
  • 🚫 Zero False Positives: Carefully tuned to handle natural movement and latency.
  • 📢 Real-time Debugging: Shows exactly at what distance a player tried to hit, making it easier for staff to monitor.
