Overview

BattleSense is a PocketMine-MP plugin that provides advanced PvP and PvE fight summaries, configurable feedback, and detailed statistics for your Minecraft Bedrock server.

  • Easy to use: Just drop it in your plugins folder!
  • Highly configurable: Choose what stats to show, how to show them, and in what language.
  • Perfect for PvP and PvE servers: Give your players meaningful feedback after every fight.

Features

  • PvP and PvE Fight Summaries:
    Players see a breakdown of damage dealt, combos, and more after every fight.

  • Configurable Output:
    Show summaries in chat, actionbar, or as a title—your choice!

  • Multi-language Support:
    All messages are customizable and translatable via language files.

Commands

Command Description Permission
/battlesense Lists all BattleSense commands battlesense.command
/battlesense about Shows plugin info battlesense.command
/battlesense reload Reloads all configs (admin only) battlesense.command.reload
