SavedInventoryPlayer is a plugin for PocketMine-MP designed to automatically save and restore player inventories when they leave and rejoin the server. This plugin ensures that all items, including tools, armor, and enchantments, are securely saved and restored without data loss.

This plugin is particularly useful for servers using plugins that remove spaces from player gamertags, such as NoNameSpaceRemover, ensuring that player inventories are not lost when they leave and then rejoin the server.

ðŸ“Œ Features

  • Automatic Saving: Player inventories, including items, tools, and armor, are automatically saved when players leave the server.
  • Automatic Restoration: Player inventories are automatically restored when players rejoin the server, ensuring they regain all their items as they were before leaving.
  • Enchantment Preservation: All enchantments on items, tools, and armor are saved and restored, so players do not lose their enchantments.
