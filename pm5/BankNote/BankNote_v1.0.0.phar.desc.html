BankNote

Turn your money into items to trade them! Right-Click to deposit a note and type /banknote {amount} to get a note.

You will need EconomyAPI Or Bedrockeconomy for this to work!

This is just a simple, but fun plugin.

Function

  • Multiple Economy.
  • Convert Your Money To Note.
  • No Config.

Command

  • /banknote {amount}
