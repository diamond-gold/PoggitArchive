General

Have you ever found yourself wanting to pick up a farmland block? Or perhaps, have you ever carried bone meal to place on fully grown crops? There are moments when these small inconveniences can feel quite significant. (That's why I created this.)

Now, you can pick up a block while preserving its original state!
Just use the pick-item function (mouse wheel) while sneaking (holding the Shift key)!

Primarily useful for pick farmland or copying chest.

Features

✔️ Block picked while keeping in its original state
✔️ Supports data copy from tile blocks. ex) chest, furance, etc...
