This plugin adds a vanilla-style fishing system to your PocketMine server. The Fishing Rod works just like in vanilla Minecraft, allowing players to fish normally.

Once the fishing hook lands in water, the fishing process begins. A fish will appear at a random time within a maximum waiting period of 60 seconds. Players must reel in the fish at the right moment to claim their reward!

Note: The Fishing Rod is not intended for PvP and can only be used for fishing.
