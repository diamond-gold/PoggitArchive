Welcome message: A plugin that is customisable and easy to use. Its as simple as installing it in your plugins folder, Restarting the server, and then editing the config to your likings.

—Features—

  1. Customisable prefix,
  2. Customisable message,
  3. Customisable server name,
  4. Customisable broadcast or whisper option.

—How to install—-

  1. Get the latest version of this plugin,
  2. put it in the plugins folder,
  3. Then restart your server,
  4. Its installed. Edit it to your likings in plugindata/WelcomeMessage/config.yml
