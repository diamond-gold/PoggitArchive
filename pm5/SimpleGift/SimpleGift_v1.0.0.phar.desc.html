SimpleGift is a lightweight plugin that allows players to gift items to other players using a simple command. Perfect for survival and economy servers, this plugin enhances player interaction and community building through item sharing.

Features

SimpleGift comes with a variety of features to enhance your server experience:

  • Simple Command: Gift items to online players with the easy-to-use /simplegift <player> or /gift <player> command
  • Security: Blacklist specific items from being gifted to prevent abuse
  • Custom Messages: Fully customizable messages with color codes and placeholders
  • Permission-based: Control who can use the gift command
  • Inventory Protection: Prevents gifting when the recipient's inventory is full
  • User Feedback: Clear messages inform both sender and recipient about gift status

Usage

Using SimpleGift is incredibly easy:

  1. Hold the item you want to gift in your hand
  2. Type /simplegift <playername> or /give <playername>
  3. The item will be transferred to the other player with confirmation messages

All commands require the simplegift.command permission.
