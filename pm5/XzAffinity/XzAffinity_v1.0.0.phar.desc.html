XzAffinity

XzAffinity is a PocketMine-MP plugin that introduces a social bonding system between players with unique relationship types such as Lovers, Siblings, and Confidents. Perfect for survival servers looking to add emotional and social depth.

Affinity Types

Type Slots per Player Description
Lovers 1 Romantic relationship
Siblings 3 Sibling-like bond
Confidents 5 Deep friendship or trusted companions
