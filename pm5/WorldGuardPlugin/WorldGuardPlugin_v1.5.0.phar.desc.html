Commands

- /rg pos Set the area. Click on the first point and block on the second point

- When you have finished setting the position, please enter /rg define [area name]

- /rg flag [area name] [flag] allow/deny,none Please enter double

- The /rg list brings out possible flags.

allow = allow
deny = Disallow - the player will be unable to interact with the type or the area.
none = none

/rg list | View the list of areas you created.

/rg addmembers [area name] [player] | Add members for the area.

/rg removemembers [area name] [player] | Remove members in the area.

/rg remove [area name] | Remove the area.

/rg info [area name] | View information for the area.

Recommended for use

It acts like a local protection.

Flag List:

build: The player will decide to install/destroy the block according to allow/deny/none.

pvp: a battle between players

tnt: TNT Explosion

invincible: Do not suffer all damage

lava-flow: lava flow

chat: player chat

use: All touches

water-flow: water flow < It will be replaced by water-flow in the next update.

tnt-damage: tnt damage

mob-damage: layer damage to entity

mob-pvp: Damage to entities

fire: Turned off when the player lights up and enters an unallowed zone

mob-spawn: Whether mobs are summoned to disallowed zones (except item entities from next update)

exit: Is the player teleportable to the area

item-drop: whether to discard the item in the area

tp: Teleport in the area Fall-damage: Whether or not the area has been damaged

Falling-blocks: Yes, sand, gravel blocks fall from the area
