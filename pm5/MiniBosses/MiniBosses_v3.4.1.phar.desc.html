Features

Configurable

  • Attributes
    • Entity type
    • Health
    • Attack damage
    • Attack range
    • Attack rate
    • Movement speed
    • Gravity
    • Scale
    • Hurt modifiers (Reduced arrow damage? No fall damage? No problem!)
    • Knockback resistance
  • Equipment
    • Held item (cosmetic)
    • Offhand (cosmetic)
    • Armor
  • Health display (Only minecraft:player)
  • Item/XP drops
    • Spread drops
  • Respawn time
  • Skin
    • Custom geometry supported
    • Cape
  • Commands on death
    • Execute as player/op/console
  • Projectiles
    • Entity type
    • Speed
    • Firing range
    • Fire rate
    • Damage
    • Explosion radius
    • Health
    • Attacking/Deflecting
    • Despawn time
    • Gravity
    • Follow the nearest player (guided projectile)
    • Particles
    • Potion effects
    • Explosive flying pigs anyone? :P
  • Top X damage rewards
    • Items
    • Commands
  • Minions
  • Many more...
