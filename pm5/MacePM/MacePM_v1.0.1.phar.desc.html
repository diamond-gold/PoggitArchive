MacePM Plugin for PocketMine-MP

MacePM is a plugin designed to bring mace weapons and wind charge items into your PocketMine-MP server! While currently in development, version 1 (v1) is available for use as the first release. Updates will follow as new features and enhancements are added.

Key Features:

  • Adds unique mace items to your PocketMine server.
  • Introduces wind charge mechanics for an extra layer of gameplay excitement.
  • Easy to install and integrate into your existing server.
