Overview

Welcome to AdvancedBounty, the ultimate bounty hunting plugin for your PocketMine-MP server. Elevate your gameplay by adding thrilling bounty hunting mechanics that allow players to place and claim bounties on others. This feature-rich plugin enhances the excitement on your server and keeps players engaged.

Note: This plugin currently uses JSON to store and modify data.

Features

Bounty Placement

Use the intuitive /bounty command to view all the available commands. Set the reward amount, and watch as the hunt begins. Will your target survive, or will someone claim the bounty?

Bounty Collection

Players with bounties on their heads become the target. Will you be the one to claim the reward by defeating them? AdvancedBounty encourages player interaction and competition.

Customization

Tailor the plugin to fit your server's unique style. Adjust bounty rewards, reward tax, and more in the easy-to-edit configuration files. Make the plugin work for you!
