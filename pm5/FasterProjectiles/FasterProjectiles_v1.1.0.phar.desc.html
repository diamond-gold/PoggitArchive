FasterProjectiles

A PocketMine-MP plugin to increase the speed of the projectiles

Tired of waiting your ender pearls to fall? Do you prefer the speed of the projectiles in Java edition? Make your ender pearls, arrows, splash potions, and all the projectiles travel faster with this plugin!

Setup

  1. Download and place the .phar file in your plugins folder.
  2. Restart your server
  3. Go to plugin_data/FasterProjectiles and set your preferred projectile speed and your preferred projectile gravity, or let it at the default value.
