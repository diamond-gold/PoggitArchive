CommandSpy

How to use?

  • Simple, just add the plugin to your server and restart it.

Description

  • This plugin gives you the ability to spy/see other players commands.
  • You can log commands in the console of your server through the config.yml.
  • You can protect certain players from being spy'ed on in the config.yml.
  • You can protect certain commands from being spy'ed on in the config.yml.
  • Make sure to restart your server after being in the config.yml.

Features?

  • Log CommandSpy to console in "config.yml"
  • Protect certain players from being spy'ed on in "config.yml"
  • Protect certain commands from being spy'ed on in "config.yml"

Permissions

  • commandspy.cmd
